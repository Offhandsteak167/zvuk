What are influencers?
Influencers exist on different platforms, they have the ability to influence potential buyers of a product or service by recommending said products via social media. An influencer needs authority, knowledge, and a relationship with their audience. They must have a large following and audience engagement. 3.484 billion people actively use social media (45% of the world)

Types of influencers

  • Mega-Influencers
    • More than 1 million followers
    • Celebrities, movie stars, sports people, musicians
    • Major brands use mega-influencers, costly, up to 1 million per post
  • Macro-Influencers
    • More accessible as influencers
    • 40 to 1 million followers
    • b-grade celebrities and online experts
    • Less expensive
    • Possible influencer fraud
  • Micro-Influencers
    • Ordinary everyday people
    • Become known for their knowledge about a niche
    • 1k to 40k followers
    • Need more convincing
  • Nano-Influencers
    • Less than 1k followers
    • Keen and interested followers who are engaging and listening to opinions
